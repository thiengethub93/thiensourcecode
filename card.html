Cat

According to a Hebrew legend, God created cats after Noah prayed for help in protecting the food stores on the Ark from being eaten by rats.

  • Scientific Name: Felis catus
  • Averages : 46 cm
  • Head-to-body length: 23-25cm
  • Weight: Between 4 and 5 kg

Cat (Felis catus) is a domestic species of small carnivorous mammal. It is the only domesticated species in the family Felidae
